Featured Food

Image Source: The Cornell Daily Sun

Fresh Apples

Vendors: Indian Creek Farm, New York Apples

Apples are a staple of the Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival. Check out the variety of types of apples at multiple vendors. Popular apples include Honeycrisp, Gala, and Fuji.

Image Source: Ksenia Verdiyan for Downtown Ithaca Alliance

Apple Cider

Vendors: Indian Creek Farm, Eve's Cidery, South Hill Cider

Apple Cider is made fresh by food vendors and can be bought by the cup, the quart, or even by the gallon.

Image Source: Ksenia Verdiyan for Downtown Ithaca Alliance

Apple Cider Donuts

Vendors: Amish, Indian Creek Farm

One of the Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival's most popular food items, apple cider donuts come in many different forms. Check out the mini cider donuts at Indian Creek Farm or a buy a box of glazed cider donuts from the Amish.

Image Source: Julia Montejo for Cornell Blogs

Caramel Apples With Whipped Cream and Nuts

Vendor: Caramel Apples (near entrance)

These caramel apples are sliced for you and topped with caramel, chocolate and nuts. Yum!

Image Source: Julia Montejo for Cornell Blogs

Apple Smoked Mac and Cheese

Vendor: Coltivare

This mac and cheese is a crowd favorite at the festival. Try it with pulled pork or get it vegetarian for a delicious spin on a classic dish.

Apple Crisp Image Source: SS Cruising Blog

Apple Crisp With Ice Cream

Vendor: Apple Crisp Stand

A classic, hot and cold treat. For sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.
